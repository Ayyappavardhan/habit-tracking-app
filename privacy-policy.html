Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to PixelStride. We believe your habits are personal, and your data should be too. We are committed to a transparency-first approach to privacy.

Local-First Promise: Your habit data lives on your device. We do not store, access, or sell your personal habit data. It remains yours, always.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Since PixelStride operates primarily offline, the information you input stays on your device:

  • Habit Details: Names, goals, and completion history.
  • Preferences: Theme settings, notification times, and app configuration.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

To improve app stability, we collect minimal anonymous data:

  • Device Info: OS version and device model (for bug fixes).
  • Anonymous Analytics: Aggregated usage stats (e.g., "Screen A visited 50 times") via standard app store analytics. No personally identifiable content is tracked.

3. Where Your Data Lives

We built PixelStride with a Zero-Knowledge Architecture regarding your personal habits.

  • Local Storage: All habit logs are stored in your device's secure sandbox.
  • Cloud Backups: If you enable iCloud or Google Drive backups, your data is encrypted and stored there, adhering to Apple's and Google's security standards. We cannot access these backups.

4. Third-Party Services

We partner with trusted providers for specific functionalities:

  • RevenueCat: Handles subscription processing securely. They process transaction receipts but do not have access to your habit data.
  • Apple/Google: Manage payment processing and app distribution.

5. Your Rights

You have complete ownership of your data:

  • Access: View your data anytime within the app.
  • Delete: Deleting the app removes all local data permanently.
  • Export: We are working on export tools to let you take your data with you.
